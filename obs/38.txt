====== 38. Və mbaʔlə Yesù ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-01.jpg?direct&}}

Every year, the Vəju celebrated the Passover. This was a celebration of how God had saved their ancestors from slavery in Egypt many centuries earlier. About three years after Yesù first began preaching and teaching publicly, Yesù gàʔ à ndzɨ̀mtə̀ sə wen la he wanted to celebrate this Passover with them in Jerusalem, and that he would be killed there.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-02.jpg?direct&}}

Və ndzàŋə̀ ndzɨ̀mtə̀ ə̀ Yesù ətsen nè Judàs . Judàs ndìʔ wìʔ a ghə̀ nwyʉə əko, a lì bɛ kòŋə̀ ə ntsoŋtə əko fà vəwenə kəmbò byì àndə ghə̀ nkòŋə̀ əko nànto. Yesù wenə ndzɨ̀mtə̀ sə wen sə nkwen li a Yèlusalèm, Judàs went to the Jewish leaders and offered to betray Yesù to them in exchange for money. He knew that the Jewish leaders denied that Yesù was the Messiah and that they were plotting to kill him.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-03.jpg?direct&}}

The Jewish leaders, led by the high priest, paid Judàs thirty silver coins to betray Yesù . This happened just as the prophets had predicted. Judàs agreed, took the money, and went away. He began looking for an opportunity to help them arrest Yesù.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù nzhɨ li kətsi kəghoʔkə wenə̀ ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen a Jerusalem . Vəwenə nwʉʔmə li ə zhɨ, Yesù nkwàʔə̀ li kəbayn kə mənkaʔə̀ ə̀ gàʔ, “Ghə̀ŋ ghaʔ. A dìʔ ə njò əwen ə̀ mò lə̀, a mà ku à ghəŋ. Ghə̀ŋ ə nnèə̀ ta lə̀ kwòʔtə̀ mò.” A dìʔ jì a Yesù nshə̀tə̀ à vəwenə la və ne zhwi ə wen byì viʔi

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù chòʔsə̀ ndoŋ ə̀ gàʔ, “Ghə̀ŋ nyʉ mèn. A dì?̀ mənlyuŋ ə́ mwomə̀ à mà kuə a məkàyn mə mfimə̀ ə shʉ̀ ə mbyɨsə viʔi. Ghə̀ŋ ə nnè tà a lə̀ nyʉə noʔə̀ mənzhʉ ntsèm kwòʔtə̀ mò.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-06.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù gàʔ à ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen , “Wìʔ fà ghə̀ŋ̧ ə ne baʔlə mò.” Kənyʉ̀ kyikə ghoʔ ndzɨmtə̀ sə Yesù , sə bə̀m la à a ne nè ndə̀ wokèn kənyʉ̀ à?. Yesù gàʔ , “A dìʔ wìʔ a kəvu kə yuwen kə kwen a kəkaŋ tà əgyayn ə̀ mùʔ.” Kəvu kə wenə̀ Judàs kə bùm .

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-07.jpg?direct&}}

After Judàs took the bread, Satan entered into him. Judàs left and went to help the Jewish leaders arrest Yesù . It was nighttime.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-08.jpg?direct&}}

Və zhɨ ə̀ mè, Yesù wenə̀ ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen sə jèʔ ə̀ djʉ̀ a pfen ə̀ Olif . Yesù gàʔ , “Ghə̀ŋ ə ne chen mò layn. Kə dìʔ a nwàʔlə̀ Nyìngòŋ la, ‘Mà ə lu ghaʔ ə ntsɨ̀ŋ ə byisə saskə.’”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-09.jpg?direct&}}

Bità bwìnsə̀ gàʔ , “kòŋ viʔi kye wù noʔə̀ vətsèm, kò mà kye!” Yesù gàʔ a Bità, “Satàn ə kəŋə kyi ghə̀ŋnoʔə̀ vətsèm, a lì mà ə jìm à wù ə Bità, la əbyɨmə ə wù ghə to kə wù à fəŋ. Even soTɛ̀ lì. mimbu nkəʔ nè toŋ a ghayn à taʔ nənchuʔə ə layn, wù ə ne tensə la wù ə ko kɨ mò.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-10.jpg?direct&}}

Bità gàʔ a Yesù , “A dìʔ tɛ̀ mpfɨ kò mà lù chen wù!” Ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen noʔə̀ sətsèm sə gàʔ ta lì .

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-11.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù mə̀ djù wenə̀  ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen a kəshi və dzàŋə̀ nə̀ Gètsemanì. Yesù gàʔ à ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen la vəwenə njɨ̀mə̀ la kə vəwenə à fəŋ a mwòmsə̀. Yesù mə̀ djʉ̀ ə jìm tà wen .

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-12.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù jɨ̀m à ghayn à taʔ , “My Father, if it is possible, please let me not have to drink this cup of suffering. But if there is no other way for people’s sins to be forgiven, then let your will be done.” Yesù was very troubled and his sweat was like drops of blood. God sent an angel to strengthen him.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù mmyètè jɨ̀mə̀ noʔə̀ əzhʉ kɔ̀ ghə̀ ə bwìnə̀ vì ə lyeə  ndzɨmtə̀ sə wen. Noʔə̀ zɛnə a ghə̀ vì , vəwenə bʉnə.Ghə̀ bwìnə̀ vì a mənzhʉ ntaʔ ə̀ gàʔ , “Ghə̀ŋ lùkə! Wìʔ yì ə vì li ə baʔlə̀ mò.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-14.jpg?direct&}}

Judàs came with the Jewish leaders, Vəsugèʔ , and a large crowd. They were carrying swords and clubs.  vì a Judàs Yesù ə̀ gàʔ , “Greetings, Teacher,” and kissed him. This was the sign for the Jewish leaders to know whom to arrest. Yesù bə̀m , “Judàs , do you betray me with a kiss?”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-15.jpg?direct&}}

Vəsugèʔ avyi ghaʔ Yesù , Peter pulled out his sword and cut off the ear of a servant of the high priest. Yesù said, “Put the sword away! I could ask the Father for an army of angels to defend me. But I must obey my Father.” Yesù bwìnsə̀ baʔsə kə̀tiʔətiʔə kə wìʔ akyì. After Yesù was arrested, ndzɨ̀mtə̀ sə wen noʔə̀ sə̀ tsèm sə nyɨŋkə.
