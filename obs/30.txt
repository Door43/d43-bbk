====== 30. Yesù nzhis li viʔi nkamsə tàyn ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù sent his apostles to preach and to teach people in many different villages. When they returned to where Yesù was, they told him what they had done. Then Yesù invited them to go with him to a quiet place across the lake to rest for a while. So, they got into a boat and went to the other side of the lake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg?direct&}}

But there were many people who saw Yesù nə̀ ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə wen leave in the boat. These people ran along the shore of the lake to get to the other side ahead of them. So when Yesù nə̀ ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə wen arrived, a large group of people was already there, waiting for them.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg?direct&}}

Ənòyn ə viʔi ayi nkìʔ lyʉmə nkamsə tàyn, kènla və taŋə kyìʔi bɛ̀nə̀ vunjə. Yesù nkusə li nshinsə à vəwenə. Yesù nyen vəwenə dìʔ tà a nə̀ byisə kɛ̀nə̀ ntsɨ̀ŋ. Ghə̀ chuʔ vəwenə, ə̀ ziʔi vəwenə ə̀ chuʔ viʔi və kwaʔkəvə à vəwenə ntìntìn.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg?direct&}}

Late in the day, the ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə wen sə gàʔ a  Yesù , “It is late and there are no towns nearby. Send the people away so they can go get something to eat.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù bòŋsə̀ gàʔ à ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə wen , “Ghə̀ŋ ku kəfo vəwenə zhɨ!” They responded, “How can we do that? We only have tàyn loaves of bread and two small fish.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù gàʔ à ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə wen vəwenə gàʔ a viʔi avyi və nwʉʔtə a nse, in groups of fifty people each.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg?direct&}}

Then Yesù took the tàyn loaves of bread and the two fish, looked up to heaven, ə̀ kù kəzoŋ ke Nyìngòŋ a əfo əzhɨvə avyi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg?direct&}}

Then Yesù broke the bread and the fish into pieces. He gave the pieces to his ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə wen to give to the people. Ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə Yesù sə fàŋ ə̀ sə̀ gə̀tə̀  əfo əzhɨvə avyi , ko və mmè! All the people ate and were satisfied.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg?direct&}}

Və mmyètə̀ li, ndzɨ̀mtə̀sə Yesù sə nkìə̀ li ə̀ byeʔ əfo əzhɨvə a və ko nzhɨ and it was enough to fill twelve baskets! All the əfo əzhɨvə avyi came from the tàyn loaves of bread and two fish.
