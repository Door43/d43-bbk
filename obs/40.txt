====== 40. Və bayntə Yesù a fəkòʔ fə kəntò ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-01.jpg?direct&}}

After the soldiers mocked Yesù, they led him away to crucify him. They made him carry fəkòʔ fə kəntò on which he would die.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-02.jpg?direct&}}

The soldiers brought Yesù to a place called “the Skull” and nailed his hands and feet to thefəkòʔ fə kəntò. Yesù gàʔ , “Tìʔ ə ghom, lyèsə̀ ku vəwenə, byì a vəwenə ko kɨ kənyʉ̀ a vəenə nèə̀.” Pilate commanded that they write, “King of the Jews” on a sign and put it  a fəkòʔ fə kəntò a kəchu ə̀ Yesù .

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-03.jpg?direct&}}

The soldiers mòʔ bàʔ nə̀ njisə Yesù . When they did this, they fulfilled a prophecy that said, “They divided my garments among them, and gambled for my clothing.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù was crucified between two robbers. One of them mocked Yesù , but the other said, “Ghə̀ŋ ə ko fwànə̀ Nyìngòŋ? We are guilty, but this man is innocent.” Ghə̀ mə̀ gàʔ a Yesù , “Please remember me in your kingdom.” Yesù gàʔ a wen, “Tà layn lə̀ wù a ndìʔ a kəshi kə fòyn ə Nyìngòŋ yuwù.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-05.jpg?direct&}}

The Jewish leaders and the other people in the crowd mocked Yesù . They said to him, “If you are the Wàyn ə̀ Nyìngòŋ , wù shiə fa fəkòʔ fə kəntò ə̀ bwòŋsə̀ əwen ə wù! Yès a byɨmə à wù.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-06.jpg?direct&}}

Then the sky over the whole region became completely dark, even though it was the middle of the day. It became dark at noon and stayed dark for three hours.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yesù mə̀ zɨmə ə̀ gàʔ, “Kə mèə̀ li! Tìʔ ə ghom, Mà ə wye li kəzhwì ə kom à wù ə kəvu.” Ghə̀ mə̀ zhə̀ŋsə̀ kəchu kə wen ə̀ pfɨ. Ghə̀ pfɨ noʔə̀ əzhʉ əkɔ̀ nse nɨ̀ʔ ə̀ njishì a sə ngə̀sə̀ viʔi fà Nyìngòŋ a ngə̀ŋ ə̀ Nyìngòŋ lì sə tinə aghayn à bò a kəntìntìn, fà gè ə̀ kwì ə̀ shiə zaʔə a gè ə̀ tèn.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-08.jpg?direct&}}

Through his death, Yesù opened a way for people to come to Nyìngòŋ . When the soldier guarding Yesù saw everything that had happened, he said, “Certainly, this man was innocent. Ghə̀ ndìʔ Wàyn ə̀ Nyìngòŋ .”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg?direct&}}

Jòsef wenə̀ Nìkòdemùs, əchu əchu ə Vəju vəbò a və mbyɨmə la Yesù ndìʔ Mèzayà, loyn kəmfɨ kə Yesù fa Palèt . Vəwenə nləm li a chì ə njisə ə̀ djʉ̀ bvɨ̀mə̀. Vəwenə mbuŋsə li mpfɨ ə̀ ngùʔ ə̀ chiʔtə ə̀ əsə̀ ə wen tsu .

//Fətitì fa nwàʔlə̀ ə̀ Nyìngòŋ////: Matthew 27:27-61; Mark 15:16-47; Luke 23:26-56; John 19:17-42//
