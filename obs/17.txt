====== 17. Məkàyn mə̀ Nyìngòŋ wenə̀ Devìd ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-01.jpg?direct&}}

Sɔl ndìʔ ə mbì fòyn ə̀ Isrèl. Ghə̀ ndìʔ kənjèf kəngɛsə̀ wìʔ tàndə viʔi Isrèlə nkəŋə. Sɔl ndìʔ noʔə̀ ngɛsə̀ fòyn a kənzìtə̀, ə̀ sə laʔtə ghə̀ kwitə ə̀ sə̀ dìʔ noʔə̀ wubyɨfə kènla ghə̀ bɛ fwànə̀ Nyìngòŋ. Nyìngòŋ tsuʔ wùkəjàŋ ə ghə̀ lu ndìʔ fòyn a kəshi ə̀ Sɔl.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-02.jpg?direct&}}

Nyìngòŋə̀ ntsuʔ fəmbvòʔ fə wìʔ və dzàŋə̀ nə̀ Devìd ə ghə̀ə ndìʔ fòyn a kəshi ə̀ Sɔl . Devìdə̀ ndìʔ ntsɨ̀ŋə̀ byisə mu ghə̀ lù fà Betòlə̀him. Ghə̀ ntsɨ̀ŋə̀ byisə tiʔə wen ə̀ zhwi nyàmə̀ kəbo bɛ̀nə̀ nyàmə̀ ntɛm a mənzhʉ mə̀ kəjàŋ mu sə̀ vì ə ghaʔ byisə wen . Devìdə̀ ndìʔ noʔə̀ ngɛs ə̀ wìʔ a ghə̀ nchoʔsə Nyìngòŋ bɛ wʉ təsoʔ tə wen.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-03.jpg?direct&}}

Devìd nkaʔ li ə̀ sə̀ dìʔ noʔə̀ sugèʔ ə to bɛ soʔə viʔi noʔə̀ nkàyn. Devìd mbɔ̀ dìʔ tà wanjə̀ ə̀ soyn Gòlayàt. Gòlayàtə̀ ndìʔ noʔə̀ kəntonto kə sugèʔ, dìʔ noʔə̀ kənjèf kə wìʔ! Alì Nyìngòŋ gyàmtə̀ Devìd ghə̀ zhwi Gòlayàt ə̀ bwòŋsə̀ viʔi Isrèl. Devìdə ntsuʔ li fa tsu ə̀ zhɨ viʔi kəbàyn ə Isrèl a əghoŋ ta kədɨ̀ŋ, və sə̀ bwomsə wen noʔə nanto.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-04.jpg?direct&}}

Sɔl nzìtə̀ li ə̀ sə̀ bànə̀ Devìd byi jì a viʔi nkòŋə̀ wen fa tsu. Sɔlə ntàŋkə̀ li aghayn aghayn ə zhwi wen, Devìd mə̀ nyiŋ ə̀ lyòmtə̀ fà Sɔl . Kətsi kətsen, Sɔl bɛ̀ sə̀ kəŋə djʉ̀ nə̀ Devìdə zhwi. Sɔl jèʔ ə̀ djʉ̀ kwen a əfyə̀ a Devìdə̀ nlyòmtə̀ tsu, alì Sɔl ko nyen ə wen. Devìdə nvi ə̀ li ə̀ boʔsə Sɔl mu ghə̀ nzì ghə̀ zhwi Sɔl alì ghə̀ kye. Devìd mbòŋsə̀ tyin ə əsoŋ ə njisə ə̀ Sɔl ti jìʔ a Sɔl la yi ə ko lu zhwi wen ti ndìʔ fòyn.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-05.jpg?direct&}}

Sɔlə nkaʔ li ə̀ pfʉ a əghoŋ, Devìd sə̀ dìʔ fòyn ə̀ Isrèl. Ghə̀ ndìʔ ə ngɛs ə̀ fòyn, viʔi kòŋə̀ wen. Nyìngòŋə mbwaʔsə li wen ə̀ nè ghə̀ soʔ viʔi ə wen a nkàyn. Devìdə nsoyn li təghoŋ ta kədɨ̀ŋ Nyìngòŋ gyàmtə̀ wen ghə̀ zhɨ viʔi kəbàyn ə̀ Isrèl. Devìdə nzhɨ li Jèlusalèm ə̀ nè a sə̀ dìʔ kəchu kə loʔ ə wen. Devìdə nnè li Isrèl kiʔi kəjoʔ nə̀ əko.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-06.jpg?direct&}}

Devìdə̀ nkəŋə ə bwom ngə̀ŋ ə̀ Nyìngòŋ a viʔi Isrèl noʔə̀ vətsèm a nkuʔsə Nyìngòŋ tsu. À nsə̀ dìʔ ngwùʔ təghɨ tə kàʔ tàndə viʔi Isrèl ə nkuʔsə̀ Nyìngòŋ a kəshi kyikə a Musìsə̀ mbwomə̀ lì.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-07.jpg?direct&}}

Alì Nyìngòŋ tɨmə nshə̀tə̀ wenə Natàn à Devìd nə̀ ntɨm yèn, “Byì tàndə wù dìʔ wìʔ a ghə̀ sonə təghoŋ, wùə ko a bwomə ngə̀ŋ Nyìngòŋ ayì. A lu bwom wayn ə wù. Alì màə bwaʔsə wu noʔə̀ nə̀mwìn. Wù ngwàʔ ə̀ wù ə tsen ə lu soʔ viʔi ə vwomə a nə̀ fòyn ə djʉ ə zaʔə a kəmyètə̀ kəmbyi!” Wù ngwàʔ Devìd ayì ndìʔ Mèzayà. Mèz ayà ndìʔ wìʔ a Nyìngòŋə̀ ntsuʔə̀, ə ghə̀ bwoŋsə viʔi mbyi venə fa mbyɨ.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-08.jpg?direct&}}

When Devìd heard these words, he immediately thanked and praised Nyìngòŋ because he had promised Devì d this great honor and many blessings. Devì d did not know when Nyìngòŋ would do these things. But as it happened, the Israelites would have to wait a long time before the Messiah came, almost 1,000 years.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-09.jpg?direct&}}

Devì d ruled with justice and faithfulness for many years, and Nyìngòŋ blessed him. However, toward the end of his life he sinned terribly against Nyìngòŋ .

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-10.jpg?direct&}}

One day, when all of Devì d soldiers were away from home fighting battles, he looked out from his palace and saw a beautiful woman bathing. Her name was Batshebà.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-11.jpg?direct&}}

Instead of looking away, Devì d sent someone to bring her to him. He slept with her and sent her back home. A short time later Batshebà sent a message to Devì d saying that she was pregnant.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-12.jpg?direct&}}

Bathsheba’s husband, a man named Uliyà , was one of Devì d best soldiers. Devì d called Uliyà back from the battle and told him to go be with his wife. But Uriah refused to go home while the rest of the soldiers were in battle. So Devì d sent Uriah back to the battle and told the general to place him where the enemy was strongest so that he would be killed.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-13.jpg?direct&}}

After Uliyà was killed, Devì d married Batshebà. Later, she gave birth to Devì d son. Nyìngòŋ was very angry about what Devì d had done, so he sent the prophet Nathan to tell Devì d how evil his sin was. Devì d repented of his sin and Nyìngòŋ forgave him. For the rest of his life, Devì d followed and obeyed Nyìngòŋ , even in difficult times.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-14.jpg?direct&}}

But as punishment for Devì d sin, his baby boy died. There was also fighting in Devì d family for the rest of his life, and Devì d power was greatly weakened. Though Devì d had been unfaithful to Nyìngòŋ , Nyìngòŋ was still faithful to his promises. Later, Devì d and Batshebà had another son, and they named him Sòlomòn.
